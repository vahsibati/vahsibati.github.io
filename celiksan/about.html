ABOUT US

Our Journey

1990

Foundation

Celiksan was established in Izmir, Turkey with the vision of manufacturing high-quality fire truck equipment.

2000

Market Expansion

Started exporting products to international markets and became one of Turkey's leading manufacturers.

2010

Innovation & Growth

Invested in new technologies and expanded product range to meet growing global demand.

2020

Global Recognition

Achieved international certifications and established presence in major markets worldwide.

Today

Industry Leader

Continuing to innovate and set industry standards in fire truck equipment manufacturing.

OUR MISSION

To give service to our customers by wide range of products based on knowledge and experience of our company that serves in Turkey more than 30 years. With the principle of reliability and honesty, to support our customers, standing by them whenever they need.

OUR VISION

To be a global company, that is among the world's leading firms, growing continuously with technological innovations, manufacturing goods according to expectations of customers, pleasing its customers and workers by enriching them and profitable to its country and society.
