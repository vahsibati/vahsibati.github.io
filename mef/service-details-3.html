PL PRODUCTION AND CONTRACT PACKAGING SERVICES

MEF Nutri Science stands as a trusted partner in contract packaging for food, dietary supplements, sports nutrition, flavors, and coloring producers across Türkiye and Europe.

We enhance our packaging services with comprehensive contract manufacturing, offering everything from bulk formulation to particle size reduction, spray drying, bulk storage, and more, ensuring your products reach the market efficiently. Each process adheres to the highest quality standards, leveraging advanced control, manufacturing, and packaging technologies for a seamless production cycle.

Our contract packaging services are as robust and meticulously monitored as our manufacturing processes, guaranteeing reliability in your product packaging. Acting as an extension of your company, we help you consistently meet all packaging requirements. We manage inventory control, printing and component production, package assembly and fulfillment, as well as warehousing and distribution.

Whether you need to meet a fast-track timeline, produce high volumes, or manage a complex packaging process, we excel at mastering projects and meeting every demand.

Our Contract Packaging Equipment

In the realm of contract packaging, we have invested in versatile equipment and developed efficient packaging processes, enabling us to offer a wide variety of packaging options with exceptional value:

  • Bag Fill Station: Our sack filling line handles plastic and kraft bags ranging from 10 kg to 25 kg. Products pass through a sieve before filling and are checked with a metal detector afterward.
  • Drum Fill Station: Our drum filler accommodates sizes from 5-gallon jugs to 55-gallon drums.
  • Consumer Packaging Line: Our packaging line features standard configurations for Horizontal (HFFS) and Vertical Form Fill Seal (VFFS) bags, with 2, 3, and 4 side sealing options, as well as bulk packaging filling lines.

Pack Line Components:

  • Vertical sachet/bag packaging
  • Horizontal sachet/bag packaging
  • Bulk/jar packaging
  • Magnets
  • Screens
  • Check weigher
  • Metal detector for aluminum packaging
  • Induction sealer
  • Ink-jet printer
  • Case erector
  • Case checkweigher
  • Box sealer

Enhance Your Packaging Supply Chain with Logistical Superiority

From our facilities and warehouses in Gebze/Kocaeli, we integrate our formulation manufacturing and contract packaging services with the strategic advantages of a major logistics hub. Our centrally located facilities enable expedited delivery across Türkiye, Europe, the Middle East, and Eurasia.
