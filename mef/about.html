About Mef Nutri Science

Founded in March 2024, our company is a dynamic start-up established by industry professionals with a mission to bring innovative products to the collagen sector. Launched as a capital-backed firm, we carry out our R&D activities at the Teknopark Istanbul Incubation Center, while our production operations take place at our facilities in the Gebze Güzeller Organized Industrial Zone.

Our management team consists of experts in dietary supplements, collagen, and functional foods, both in Turkey and globally. With deep expertise in encapsulated product technology, our team has developed cutting-edge encapsulated collagen products, positioning us at the forefront of innovation and competition in this field.

Beyond collagen production, we specialize in particle engineering and contract manufacturing, providing high-quality solutions tailored to industry needs. Our commitment to innovation and excellence drives us to shape the future of the collagen industry.
