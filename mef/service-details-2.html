SPRAY DRYING IN CONTRACT MANUFACTURING

Spray drying converts a liquid raw material into a powdered product by water evaporation. At MEF NUTRI SCIENCE, we excel in spray drying, a cutting-edge process that converts moist or liquid raw materials into encapsulated, granulated and powdered products. This method is essential in the food, flavor, and agro industries for creating soluble beverage powders and granulates.

As a premier outsourcing specialist, MEF NUTRI SCIENCE provides extensive spray drying services for industrial production. We are committed to developing bespoke manufacturing processes tailored to your specific needs. Reach out to us today to explore your project possibilities!

"By adding spray drying technology to our existing particle engineering services, MEF NUTRI SCIENCE offers customers innovative solutions to manufacture food intermediates and enhance the texture, sensory properties, and stability of their products. MEF can assist from laboratory scale to commercial production, from 50 grams to metric tonnes.

MEF NUTRI SCIENCE is able to dry and return your product quickly. We aim to turn around all products within 7 days, and often even sooner, working day and night to help you meet your deadlines. Our team specializes in the food and ingredients industry, making us a competent, problem-solving, and highly specialized company. We offer the processes needed for the drying of water-soluble extracts, suspensions, aqueous solutions, single components, multi-component mixes, and emulsions.

Whether you're looking for small lab-scale trial runs or require regular production of larger quantities, our company is here to help. We handle jobs of all types and sizes and will work with companies of all scales to provide the exact service you need.

Our spray drying technology for powder drying or general drying processes includes mixing, dispersion of various substances (liquids and/or powders). We also offer various processing options such as heating, homogenizing, or cooling. Our specialized spray drying towers with external fluid beds meet all food safety requirements and are equipped with jet dispersion nozzles. We can optionally perform partial agglomeration or post-agglomeration of fine material, followed by cooling via fluid beds.

The minimum quantity for our services starts from kilograms, and we offer packaging options in paper bags, films, and drums in sizes ranging from 3 grams to 25 kg, or in big bags from 500 to 1000 kg.

Outsourcing spray drying can be a cost-effective alternative to purchasing equipment. Benefits include certified quality, state-of-the-art technology, and demand-oriented production.

MEF NUTRI SCIENCE offers a range of contract manufacturing services, specializing in the drying of powders and granulates. We are ready to develop customized drying processes for your products. Contact us today to learn more!
